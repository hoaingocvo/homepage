About Us

This store owned by Hoai Ngoc Vo, a Viet content creator who speacialized in ancient history and culture. She is also the co-funder of a well-known research non-profit organization in Vietnam - Quintessence of Dai Viet or @daivietphonghoa.

Question and Answer

How long is the shipping?

Pre-order products shipped from Vietnam took 4 - 6 weeks. Instock products took under 1 week.

How does the renting works?

Rented products only available in person or in-state - Virginia. You will submit your ID in holds until the pick-up date.

Price for all rent items are fixed rate 30% of original price. Embroided products are 50% of original price. Damaged rented products are subject to 100% price paid.

How does the payment works?

After submiting your order and information, you will received an email or text messages with your order number. You will confirm the prices and items in your order before sending the payment to me through Zelle, Bank transfer or Paypal with your order number + name. You will receive a text confirm your payment and expected deliver date.
